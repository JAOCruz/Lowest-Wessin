About CANA LAW SELECTION

CANA LAW SELECTION is a specialized legal practice founded by Gonzalo J. Sánchez Modesto, Attorney at Law, with over 10 years of experience in Dominican Republic real estate law. Our mission is to protect international buyers and investors by providing comprehensive legal due diligence and guidance throughout the property purchase process.

We understand the unique challenges of buying property in the Dominican Republic, where real estate agents are not regulated and contracts are often drafted to favor sellers. That's why we focus exclusively on real estate transactions, ensuring every property we recommend is one we'd buy for ourselves or someone we care about.

Our comprehensive approach includes title verification, developer vetting, contract negotiation, and secure closing guidance. We work across all major regions including Punta Cana, Santo Domingo, Santiago, Puerto Plata, and Samaná.

Unlike some countries, the Dominican Republic does not have title insurance, making independent legal verification critical. We perform thorough due diligence to confirm properties are free of liens, mortgages, or legal disputes, and verify all permits and approvals are in order.

Whether you're investing for rental income, relocating permanently, or building a family legacy, our experienced team ensures you can invest with confidence, clarity, and peace of mind. Every client receives personalized attention and comprehensive legal protection throughout their real estate journey.
