Why Our Process Matters

The Dominican Republic real estate market lacks the regulatory protections found in developed markets. Our comprehensive process ensures you're protected every step of the way.

No Title Insurance

Unlike the US, the DR doesn't use title insurance. Our due diligence replaces this protection.

Unregulated Agents

Real estate agents don't require licensing, making independent legal verification essential.

Complex Legal System

Navigating Dominican law requires specialized expertise and local knowledge.
