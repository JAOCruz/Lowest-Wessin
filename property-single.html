Las Terrenas Villa, Samaná

El Jamito, Las Terrenas, Samaná, Dominican Republic

Beautiful two-level villa with clear title and updated boundaries, featuring spectacular views of the ocean, Las Terrenas town, and mountains. Located in El Jamito, 500 meters from the Sánchez–Las Terrenas highway, and just 10 minutes from the best beaches and downtown.

Total size 298 m² (105 m² interior, 32 m² terrace, 161 m² garden and parking). Modern, functional layout with potential for expansion. Surrounded by nature with refreshing microclimates and sunshine.

  • Bathrooms: 2.5 (includes guest half bath)
  • Total Area: 298 m²
  • Features: Pool, laundry area, equipment room, rear garden, outdoor kitchen, front garden, parking
